Origins of Onra

Onra Designs Limited was established on January 14TH, 2008. Onra is a locally registered company based in Nairobi's Central Business District(CBD) and is incorporated under the Company's Act(Cap. 486).

Over the years, Onra Designs Limited has established a strong presence in the graphic design industry, with a particular focus on Brand Identity. This success is driven by the expertise and creativity of our Creative Director, whose extensive experience has shaped the company's innovative approach. Through a deep understanding of design principles and market trends, we have become known for crafting memorable and impactful brand identities that resonate with our clients and their audiences.
